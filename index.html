Wiki story:



Wiki Story is a story generation game where players create a story collaboratively, making use of the ‘generate random article’ button on Wikipedia.

Players take turns to add to the story, copying a portion of a sentence from a random article. The enjoyment of the game comes from trying to make meaning out of random content, finding creative ways to join things together.

The story is finished when players feel like it has reached the end - this will usually be after a paragraph or two. Players can then read the story out loud to one another.



Rules:



  • 2 players, turn-based
  • A new article is used for each turn
  • If the article contains very little text (e.g. only a sentence-long), players are allowed to generate a new one
  • Players should copy and paste text from their article onto the shared doc/website
  • The length of the text is up to the player, but should be at least two words and not more than a full sentence long
  • Punctuation is ‘free’ - meaning that players can edit the last punctuation used in the story, and add their own at the end of their addition
  • The players decide amongst themselves when the story is finished
  • They can then read the story out loud to one another
